आळंदी विवाह संस्था - Complete Marriage Guide

Jun 30 2026

Choosing the right path for marriage is one of the most significant decisions in a person's life. As traditional ceremonies evolve, many couples prefer legal, streamlined, and hassle-free solutions like Registered or Court Marriages. Aalandi Vivah Sanstha (आळंदी विवाह संस्था) stands out as a premiere institution providing complete legal guidance and seamless event management for couples embarking on their lifelong journey.

Why Choose Aalandi Vivah Sanstha Only?

Aalandi Vivah Sanstha is uniquely optimized to manage all administrative, regulatory, and ceremonial requirements. The bureau provides comprehensive end-to-end management for all forms of legal unions, including:

  • Registered Arrange Marriage: Giving traditional parental alliances a firm legal foundation.
  • Court Arrange Marriage: Formalized courtroom unions executed legally under appropriate civil laws.
  • Registered Love Marriage & Court Love Marriage: Offering legal security and complete documentation for self-chosen unions.
  • Live-in Relationship Documentation: Safe, secure, and legally protected documentation solutions.

Required Documentation and Witnesses

To ensure a completely smooth registration process under the Hindu Marriage Act of 1955 or civil registration rules, specific documentation is required from the groom, bride, and witnesses:

Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhar Card: Mandatory for the Groom, Bride, and exactly 3 Witnesses.

Age Proof (Any 2 of the following 4 options for Groom & Bride)

  • School Leaving Certificate (LC / TC)
  • Birth Certificate
  • 10th Marksheet / Board Certificate
  • Bonafide Certificate or Pan Card

Photographs

  • Groom: 4 Passport Size Photos
  • Bride: 4 Passport Size Photos
  • Witnesses: 1 Passport Size Photo each

Premium Facilities Provided As Per Demand

Aalandi Vivah Sanstha handles all standard documentation as well as comprehensive ceremonial packages. Options include:

  • Full Vedic & Legal Ceremonies: Traditional Hindu Vedic marriages conducted seamlessly by professional Brahmins inside proper Marriage Halls.
  • Complete Hospitality Management: High-quality catering services, traditional photography, wedding garlands (लग्नांचे हार), groom's traditional attire accents (नवरदेवाचा फेटा), and ceremonial items (मुंडावळ्या).
  • Police Protection Applications: Submission of official legal protection applications for peace of mind.
  • Immediate Certificate Assistance: Direct oversight to ensure standard registration processing without delay.

Unmatched Privacy, Counseling & Post-Marriage Trust

Aalandi Vivah Sanstha places immense priority on relationship sustainability and privacy:

  • Strict Secrecy and Confidentiality: Absolute privacy is maintained for love marriages and live-in relationships.
  • Continuous Feedback and Counseling: The relationship support doesn't end with the certificate. Frequent follow-ups are taken for 3 years at intervals of every 6 months, offering counseling at highly affordable rates if any familial conflicts arise.
